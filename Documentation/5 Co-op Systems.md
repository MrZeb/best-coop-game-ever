Since co-op is your first pillar, dedicate a section to it.

Ask:

### How does cooperation create value?

Bad answer:

> Two people can hit enemies faster.

Good answer:

> One player navigates while another interprets clues.

Examples:

- Shared map-making
- Multi-person puzzles
- Resource specialization
- Rescue mechanics
- Information asymmetry

A good test:

> Would the game still be interesting if a second player was replaced with an AI?

If yes, your co-op systems probably need more depth.
