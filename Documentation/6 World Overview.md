# World Design

Keep this high-level initially.

### Questions

- What makes the world dangerous?
- Why are players exploring it?
- Is it fantasy, sci-fi, post-apocalyptic?
- What mysteries exist?
- How much is procedurally generated?

Example:

|Category|Notes|
|---|---|
|Setting|Ancient flooded continent|
|Main Threat|Wildlife and environmental hazards|
|Mystery|Lost civilization|
|Exploration Driver|Discover technology and routes|
|Tone|Lonely but beautiful|
