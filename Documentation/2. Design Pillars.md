# The Core Pillars

For each pillar, define what it means and what it forbids.

### 1. Co-op Focus

**Means:**

- Players can solve problems together.
- Information sharing is valuable.
- Different players may notice different things.

**Forbids:**

- One player doing everything.
- Solo play being strictly better.
- Mechanics that isolate players for long periods.

---

### 2. Dangerous World

**Means:**

- Mistakes matter.
- Travel carries risk.
- Resources have value.

**Forbids:**

- Infinite healing.
- Consequence-free exploration.
- Enemies that are merely speed bumps.

---

### 3. No Handholding

**Means:**

- Players learn by observing.
- Tutorials are mostly diegetic.
- Information is incomplete.

**Forbids:**

- Giant arrows.
- Quest GPS markers.
- Explaining every mechanic immediately.

---

### 4. Discovery First

**Means:**

- Hidden mechanics.
- Emergent solutions.
- Secrets worth finding.

**Forbids:**

- Revealing all recipes immediately.
- Showing every point of interest on the map.
- Solving mysteries for the player.
